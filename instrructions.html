Memory Game Instructions

The goal of this game is to match all pairs of cards within the given time limit.

  1. Click on two cards at a time to reveal their images.
  2. Matching cards will remain face-up, while mismatched cards will flip back.
  3. Keep track of the cards you've seen to find matches more quickly.
  4. The timer will count down from 120 seconds. If you don't match all cards before time runs out, you lose.
